Bachpan Express

The joy of learning, a fast-paced journey – every experience matters!

Bachpan Express is our unique education program designed to transform weekends into a time of creativity, learning, and growth for students. Every Saturday and Sunday, our dedicated teachers and volunteers come together to provide school children with engaging and enriching experiences that go beyond traditional academics.

What Makes Bachpan Express Special?

Our program focuses on the holistic development of students, emphasizing skill-building, well-being, and joyful learning through activities such as:

Character Building

Instilling good habits and teaching children to differentiate between positive and negative behaviors.

Creative Exploration

Music and Dance: Enabling students to express themselves and explore cultural richness.

Art and DIY Crafts: Inspiring creativity through painting, sketching, and hands-on projects.

Healthy Living Practices

Teaching the importance of cleanliness, hygiene, and nutrition for a healthier lifestyle.

Tech Awareness

Introducing children to the responsible use of technology to prepare them for the digital world.

A Unique Learning Environment

Engaging Sessions

Designed to make learning fun and interactive, ensuring students are excited to attend every session.

Focus on Mental and Physical Well-Being

Activities that enhance mental health, mindfulness, and overall happiness are integral to the program.

Skill Enhancement

Preparing students with essential life skills to boost their confidence and equip them for future challenges.

Impact of Bachpan Express

Through Bachpan Express, we aim to create confident, creative, and skilled individuals who value their well-being and are ready to embrace life with positivity. This initiative is more than just a program—it’s a movement to inspire and empower the next generation.

Join us in shaping the future, one weekend at a time!
