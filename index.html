About Us

RIPULL FOUNDATION (CIN: U88900DL2024NPL438823) is a non-governmental organization committed to making a meaningful impact on society. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through comprehensive programs focused on livelihood, education, redevelopment, and health. We believe in creating sustainable change and offering opportunities for growth and well-being to those in need.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in social transformation, creating a world where every individual has the opportunity to live a dignified, healthy, and prosperous life.

Our Mission

To design and implement impactful programs in collaboration with communities and stakeholders that address critical challenges in livelihood, education, health, and overall redevelopment of people's lives.

Our Work

Livelihood

We focus on creating sustainable livelihood opportunities through skill development, vocational training, and support for small enterprises, enabling individuals and families to achieve economic independence.

Education

Our educational initiatives aim to provide access to quality learning for underprivileged children and adults, fostering knowledge, skills, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Redevelopment

We work towards the redevelopment of people's lives and communities by improving infrastructure, promoting sustainable practices, and empowering communities to lead their own development.

Health

Our health programs are designed to improve access to healthcare services, promote health awareness, and address critical health issues, ensuring the well-being of the communities we serve.
